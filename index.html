      City of Raleigh
      Parks, Recreation and Cultural Resources
      2015 Bond Projects


      The parks bond will be used to improve existing parks and recreation facilities as well as investment in new infrastructure. Projects include greenways, community centers, historic sites, pools and aquatic facilities, arts centers, athletic parks, playgrounds, land acquisition and more. Implementation will take place over the next five to seven years. Phase one will begin Fall 2015.
